How to Play

Welcome! Click on the button "Play" and type the name of an animal you see in the box under it. When you are finished press "enter" or click on the "check" button to get your score! If you are not sure you can click on the "help" button, it will correct the first error or add the first missing letter, but it will give you no score, you can only use 5 helps during our game. If you type fast you will get an extra life. Click the "next" button to get to the next level.

The game will end when you have seen all the animals or when you have lost all your lives. After the game ends your score will be added to a global chart and to a list of your scores. You will be able to check them by clicking respectively on the "Chart" or "Your Scores" buttons.

You will be able to change your personal data in any moment, including: profile picture, name, e-mail, age, gender, password or even delete your account. To do that just click on the "Profile" button.
